DESCRIPTION OF BUSINESS
 
We were incorporated in the State of Nevada on February 15, 2017. We manufacture and sell drywall steel studs. Our product is used principally in new developments, commercial and residential construction and in home improvement, remodeling and repair work. We distribute our drywall steel studs in the Kyrgyz market to wholesale customers. In the future, when/if we have available resources, operating history and experience, we plan to expand our market to China, Europe and Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS) countries.
